Purisima Creek Redwoods

Redwood hike

Purisima Creek Trail · Purisima Creek Redwoods Open Space Preserve.

Lush coastal redwoods — pick your vibe:

💪 Workout · Whittemore Gulch Steep gulch between redwood canyon and open ridge with ocean views. ~2.4 mi one-way · ~1,500 ft down from Skyline
Out-and-back ~4.8 mi · ~1,500 ft climb back Full loop w/ Purisima Creek: ~9 mi · ~1,600–2,100 ft
🌿 Chill · Purisima Creek Trail Easy, shaded walk along the creek — go as far as you like. Mostly flat · out-and-back, your call on distance Turn around whenever you feel like it.
